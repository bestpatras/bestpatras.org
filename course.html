BEST Local Course in Patras 2021

Extraordinary Nanomaterials for a New Megaworld

From July 10th till 19th, 19 STEM students from the University of Patras will be attending high level seminars and workshops by professors of our University with expertise in the field of nanomaterials.

Learn from the experts

Under the supervision of professor Konstantinos Dassios from the Department of Chemical Engineering, besides lectures the participants will join the experts in the labs! This will be the perfect chance for them to apply their theoretical knowledge and see how nanomaterials are transformed into unique and practical assets from the perspective of an investigator.
